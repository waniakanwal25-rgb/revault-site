Revault · Guide

Caring for Thrifted Pieces

Pre-loved fashion deserves a little extra love. Follow these simple steps to keep your pieces looking their best for years to come.

  • Arrives Clean & Ready
    Every piece is washed and pressed before it leaves us — so the moment it reaches you, it's ready to wear. No prep needed, just put it on.
  • Store With Care
    Fold knitwear flat to avoid stretching. Hang structured pieces on padded hangers. Store in a cool, dry place away from plastic bags that trap moisture.

Return & Refund Policy

At Revault, all sales are considered final. We take great care in curating and quality-checking every piece before it reaches you.

Returns are only accepted in the following circumstances:

  • — The item arrived damaged or defective
  • — An incorrect item was sent to you

Any such issue must be reported within 24 hours of receiving your order. Please reach out via Instagram with your order details and a photo of the item.

Claims raised after 24 hours of delivery will not be eligible for return or refund.
